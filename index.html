Frequently Asked Questions

How can I buy bus ticket from ticketlanka?

You can use our website to buy bus tickets from ticketlanka.com. After entering the place and date you want to travel, you can compare all the trips and buy your ticket by entering your passenger and card information for the trip that suits you.

Is ticketlanka.com reliable?

You can buy your ticket safely on ticketlanka.com. The purchases you make on our site are secured with an SSL certificate. In addition, 3D Security assurance is offered at the payment step when purchasing a bus ticket.

Is commision paid when buying ticket from ticketlanka.com?

ticketetlanka.com does not charge commission. When buying a bus ticket using ticketetlanka.com, you only pay the price of your ticket.

How can I cancel my bus ticket?

You can cancel your bus ticket in accordance with the cancellation conditions determined by the companies. Cancellation conditions are communicated to you at the time of purchase.

When will I be refunded when I cancel my bus ticket?

After your cancellation is confirmed, the refund will be transferred to your bank without any deductions. The amount you paid will be reflected in your account within 7 working days, depending on your bank's return procedures. In transactions made with debit cards, this process may take 1-20 business days. The return process is completely dependent on your bank and ticketetlanka.com has no right to intervene.
